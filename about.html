A Little Bit About Me

My name is Anthony Castelli, I originally grew up in Southern Illinois near St. Louis, but went to high school near Chicago. I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in the spring of 2015 with a bachelor's degree in Atmospheric Science. While in college I found I liked to program, and tried on numerous occasions to teach myself. However, by the time I graduated I still didn't really feel like I had enough experience with one language to get a job doing something interesting. So, I applied to the Dev Bootcamp program and moved to Redwood City, California to start my new life as a web developer!

Some interesting things about me include:

  • I started college in electrical engineering, and while I ended up graduating in a totally different field, I still do a lot of side projects and learning related to EE
  • As far back as I can remember I've been interested in plants and gardening, and I try to keep as many plants around me as I can
    • Currently, I'm cultivating several kinds of succulents along with some carnivorous plants (A first for me!)
  • I really like computer games, and my ideal job would involve making web-based games
  • For DBC: Something quirky about me is that I still have my original Gamecube from 2001, and about once a year I pull it out and play through all of Pikmin 1 and 2
